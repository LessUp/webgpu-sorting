ℹ️ What you are measuring

Bitonic Sort

A comparison-based GPU sorting network. Useful as the easiest implementation path to inspect and reason about.

Radix Sort

A non-comparison integer sorter for Uint32Array workloads. It uses histogram-style passes and tends to shine on larger arrays.
